## About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the [Beijing Institute of Technology](https://bit.edu.cn/), supervised by [Prof. Wei Liang](http://pie-lab.cn/) and Prof. Feng Liu. Earlier, I obtained my master's degree from [Jilin University](https://www.jlu.edu.cn/) and completed my undergraduate studies at [Hefei University of Technology](https://www.hfut.edu.cn/).

My research interests include Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Object Detection, and Multi-Modal Environment Perception.
